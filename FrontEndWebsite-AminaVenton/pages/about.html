About

This blog is about nail polish. I started painting my nails a few years ago as a total beginner. I realized I wanted to take pictures of the swatches and document my progress along the way! I am not an expert by any means, but I have tried a few nail art techniques like nail stamping and hand-drawn stickers. Painting my nails has given me a lot of joy over the years, and I am glad I get to share it with you!
